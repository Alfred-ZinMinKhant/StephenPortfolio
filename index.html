About Me

Passionate about creating meaningful digital experiences

Hello, I'm Min Thadan

I am a driven and versatile digital marketing professional with hands-on experience across account management, social media strategy, influencer coordination, and research-driven content planning. Over the years, I have worked closely with brands, creators, and clients to build strong online presences, develop compelling content, and execute campaigns that align with clear business objectives.

My background includes managing high-volume social media accounts, conducting brand audits, developing customer personas, analyzing market trends, and delivering strategic insights through detailed presentations and reports. I excel at bridging creativity with data—leading content creation, coordinating teams, organizing events, and ensuring smooth communication across all stakeholders.

With a strong focus on relationship-building, problem-solving, and efficiency, I bring a proactive and adaptable approach to every project. I am passionate about creating meaningful digital experiences and helping brands grow through thoughtful strategies and impactful storytelling.
